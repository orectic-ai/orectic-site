2. Description of Services

2.1 The Orectic Platform

Orectic is an AI-powered autonomous intelligence operating system that processes unstructured business data into structured, actionable intelligence. The platform consists of:

  • Intelligence Extraction Pipeline: Ingests business data across multiple file types (transcripts, documents, emails, audio, video, PDFs, spreadsheets, and other formats) and extracts structured intelligence across multiple categories including people, businesses, opportunities, commitments, frameworks, problems, alerts, and content.
  • Oracle Agent System: An autonomous AI agent that reasons over extracted intelligence to answer questions, surface insights, and execute operational tasks on behalf of the client, delivered through messaging channels including Telegram, with additional channels on the roadmap.
  • Command Center Dashboard: A web-based interface at app.orectic.ai where extracted intelligence is browsable, searchable, and exportable.
  • Integration Layer: Connections to third-party tools and services (CRM, email, calendar, payment systems, and others) that allow the Oracle to cross-validate intelligence against live data and take authorized actions.
