Privacy Policy

Orectic, LLC  ·  orectic.ai | app.orectic.ai
Effective Date: April 13, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Orectic, LLC ("Orectic," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our websites at orectic.ai and app.orectic.ai (collectively, the "Services"), use our AI-powered intelligence platform, or otherwise interact with us.

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not access or use our Services.

Orectic is headquartered in Austin, Texas. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our Services regardless of location.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, job title, phone number, and password when you create an account on app.orectic.ai.
  • Business Data: Transcripts, documents, emails, audio files, video files, spreadsheets, PDFs, and other business data that you upload to or connect with the Orectic platform for processing through our intelligence extraction pipeline. This is the core data you provide for the platform to analyze.
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details, and transaction history. Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment processor. We do not store complete credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Communications: Information you provide when you contact us for support, provide feedback, or communicate with us through email, chat, or other channels.
  • Intake and Onboarding Data: Responses to discovery questionnaires, business context forms, and onboarding materials provided during the deployment process.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access our Services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, actions taken within the platform, search queries, Oracle interactions, and time spent on various sections of the Services.
  • Device and Browser Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, screen resolution, and language preferences.
  • Log Data: Server logs that record requests made to our servers, including timestamps, referring URLs, and error information.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, local storage, and similar tracking technologies as described in Section 9.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Integrations

When you connect third-party services to Orectic (such as CRM systems, email providers, calendar services, communication platforms, or financial tools), we access and process data from those services as authorized by you. The types of data accessed depend on the specific integrations you enable and the permissions you grant. We only access data necessary to provide the Services you have requested.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Services: Processing your business data through our intelligence extraction pipeline, generating structured intelligence outputs, powering Oracle agent interactions, delivering the Command Center dashboard, and providing the core functionality of the platform.
  • Account Management: Creating and managing your account, authenticating your identity, and maintaining your subscription.
  • Platform Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve the performance, reliability, and functionality of our Services. This may include analyzing aggregated, de-identified usage data to understand how the platform is used.
  • Communication: Sending you service-related notifications, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages. With your consent, sending marketing communications about new features, products, or services.
  • Security: Detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, security incidents, and technical issues.
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and governmental requests.
  • Billing and Payments: Processing payments, managing subscriptions, and providing invoices and receipts.

3.1 How We Handle Your Business Data

Your business data (transcripts, documents, emails, and other files you upload or connect to the platform) is the core asset you entrust to Orectic. We want to be explicit about how we handle it:

  • Your business data is processed through our extraction pipeline to generate structured intelligence for your use.
  • Your business data is stored in tenant-isolated infrastructure. Each client's data is logically separated from every other client's data.
  • We do not sell your business data to third parties.
  • We do not use your business data to train general-purpose AI models.
  • We do not share your raw business data with other clients.
  • Access to your business data within Orectic is restricted to authorized personnel who need access to provide the Services.

3.2 Cross-Tenant Learning

Orectic may analyze aggregated, de-identified patterns across tenants to improve platform performance, extraction accuracy, and operational intelligence. This analysis operates on statistical patterns and metadata — not on your raw business data. For example, we may analyze aggregated extraction accuracy rates across all tenants to improve our pipeline, but we will never share the specific contents of your transcripts or documents with another client. You may opt out of cross-tenant pattern analysis by contacting us at privacy@orectic.ai.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your active subscription plus 90 days after account closure, unless you request earlier deletion.
  • Business Data: Retained for the duration of your active subscription. Upon termination of your subscription, your business data will be retained for 30 days to allow for export, after which it will be permanently deleted from our systems, including all extracted intelligence, vector embeddings, and normalized data.
  • Extracted Intelligence: Structured intelligence generated from your business data (entities, relationships, commitments, opportunities, etc.) is retained for the duration of your subscription and deleted with your business data upon termination.
  • Usage and Log Data: Retained for up to 12 months for security, debugging, and platform improvement purposes.
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Communications: Support communications and correspondence retained for up to 24 months.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting privacy@orectic.ai. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any legal retention requirements.

6. Data Security

We implement and maintain reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

  • Tenant-isolated data architecture: Each client's data is logically separated using per-tenant storage paths and access controls across our infrastructure (Cloudflare R2, Vectorize, and Railway).
  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at rest: Data stored on Cloudflare R2 and Neon PostgreSQL is encrypted at rest using AES-256 or equivalent.
  • Authentication: User authentication is managed through Clerk with industry-standard session management and token handling.
  • Access controls: Internal access to client data is restricted on a need-to-know basis and logged for audit purposes.
  • Infrastructure security: Our cloud infrastructure provides built-in DDoS protection, rate limiting, and edge security across all deployment surfaces.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have reason to believe your account or data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@orectic.ai.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format. Orectic supports data export from the Command Center dashboard.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You have the right to opt out of marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us.
  • Opt-Out of Cross-Tenant Analysis: You have the right to opt out of aggregated cross-tenant pattern analysis as described in Section 3.2.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where we process your information based on consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@orectic.ai. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing a request.

7.1 Texas Data Privacy and Security Act

If you are a Texas resident, you may have additional rights under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), effective July 1, 2024, including the right to know what personal data is collected, the right to delete personal data, the right to correct inaccuracies, the right to data portability, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal data, targeted advertising, and profiling. Orectic does not sell personal data. To exercise your TDPSA rights, contact privacy@orectic.ai.

7.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, disclosed, or sold; the right to delete personal information; the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information; and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights. Orectic does not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA. To exercise your rights, contact privacy@orectic.ai.

7.3 European Economic Area, UK, and International Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions with data protection laws, you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent legislation. If GDPR applies to your use of our Services, our lawful bases for processing are: (a) performance of a contract (providing the Services you subscribed to); (b) legitimate interests (platform improvement, security); and (c) consent (marketing communications). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. For data protection inquiries, contact privacy@orectic.ai.
